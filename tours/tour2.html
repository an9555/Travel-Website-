      Day 1: Arrival and Acropolis

    • Arrive in Athens and settle into your accommodation.
    • Begin your journey at the Acropolis, exploring the Parthenon, Erechtheion, and other ancient structures.
    • Visit the Acropolis Museum to delve into the history and artifacts of ancient Athens.

      Day 2: Ancient Agora and Plaka

    • Discover the Ancient Agora, the heart of ancient Athens, and explore the Temple of Hephaestus and Stoa of Attalos.
    • Stroll through the picturesque streets of Plaka, the historic neighborhood filled with traditional shops and tavernas.

      Day 3: Cooking Class and Beach Relaxation

    • Take a cooking class to learn the secrets of Greek cuisine, preparing dishes like moussaka, souvlaki, or tzatziki.
    • Enjoy a delicious lunch featuring your creations.
    • Spend the afternoon relaxing on one of Athens' nearby beaches, such as Vouliagmeni or Glyfada.

      Day 4: National Archaeological Museum and Mount Lycabettus

    • Visit the National Archaeological Museum, home to a vast collection of Greek antiquities.
    • Marvel at ancient treasures like the Mask of Agamemnon and the Antikythera Mechanism.
    • Climb Mount Lycabettus for stunning panoramic views of Athens.

      Day 5: Cape Sounion and Sunset

    • Take a trip to Cape Sounion, where the Temple of Poseidon stands majestically overlooking the Aegean Sea.
    • Enjoy breathtaking views and witness a magical sunset.
    • Return to Athens for a farewell dinner, savoring Greek delicacies.
    • This revised itinerary provides a well-rounded experience in Athens, allowing you to explore heritage sites, immerse yourself in Greek history at museums, savor the local cuisine through a cooking class, and relax on the nearby beaches. Feel free to adjust the activities based on your preferences and available time. Enjoy your trip to Athens!
