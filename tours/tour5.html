    Here's a simplified version of the suggested trail to travel around Corfu:

    Day 1: Corfu Town - Explore the historic Old Town, visit Achilleion Palace.

    Day 2: Northern Coast - Visit Paleokastritsa, Sidari, and Kassiopi.

    Day 3: Eastern Coast and Mount Pantokrator - Discover Barbati, Ipsos, and hike Mount Pantokrator.

    Day 4: Southern Coast and Corfu's Interior - Explore Agios Gordios, Glyfada, Pelekas, and traditional villages.

    Day 5: Western Coast and Sunset Views - Visit Afionas, Agios Georgios, and enjoy sunset in Arillas.

    This simplified itinerary covers the highlights of Corfu, allowing you to experience its historical sites, beautiful beaches, scenic landscapes, and charming villages. Feel free to customize it based on your preferences and available time. Enjoy your journey!
