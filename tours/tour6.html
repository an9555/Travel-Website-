      Day 1: Arrival and Exploring Fira

    • Arrive in Santorini and settle into your accommodation in the capital, Fira.
    • Spend the day exploring Fira's charming streets, lined with shops, restaurants, and cafes.
    • Visit the Museum of Prehistoric Thera to learn about the island's ancient history and the eruption of the volcano that shaped Santorini.

      Day 2: Discovering Oia and Caldera Views

    • Head to the iconic village of Oia, famous for its white-washed buildings and blue-domed churches.
    • Explore the narrow cobblestone streets, browse through boutique shops, and take in the breathtaking views of the Caldera.
    • Experience a mesmerizing Santorini sunset from one of the picturesque viewpoints.

      Day 3: Volcano and Hot Springs

    • Embark on a boat tour to explore the volcanic islands of Nea Kameni and Palea Kameni.
    • Hike to the crater of the volcano and witness the unique landscape.
    • Enjoy a refreshing swim in the hot springs of Palea Kameni.

      Day 4: Beaches and Akrotiri Archaeological Site

    • Visit the ancient Minoan city of Akrotiri, known as the "Pompeii of the Aegean," to explore the well-preserved ruins.
    • Relax and unwind on the beautiful beaches of Perissa or Kamari.
    • Indulge in water activities like swimming, sunbathing, or even trying out some water sports.

      Day 5: Winery Tour and Sunset in Fira

    • Take a tour of the island's wineries and learn about Santorini's unique vineyards and wine production.
    • Enjoy wine tasting sessions and sample the local varieties.
    • End your trip with a memorable Santorini sunset from Fira, savoring the breathtaking views and reflecting on your wonderful journey.
    This itinerary allows you to experience the best of Santorini, from its picturesque villages and stunning views to its archaeological sites and beautiful beaches. Feel free to adjust the activities based on your interests and preferences. Enjoy your trip to Santorini!
